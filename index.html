Tracked Targets

Configured Target Set

  • MSH3 · MutS Homolog 3
    Part of MutSbeta complex. Recognizes CAG repeat loops. Rate-limiting: 50% reduction = 50% less expansion.
  • FAN1 · Fanconi Anemia-Associated Nuclease 1
    Protective nuclease that opposes somatic expansion. Strongest genetic association to delayed onset.
  • PMS1 · Post-Meiotic Segregation 1
    MutL family member. Splice modulators can reduce PMS1, suppressing expansion while preserving cancer-protective MMR.
  • MLH1 · MutL Homolog 1
    Forms MutLgamma endonuclease with MLH3. Nicks DNA opposite CAG loops, enabling expansion.
  • LIG1 · DNA Ligase 1
    Seals nicks during repair, locking in expansion. K845N variant delays onset by 7-8 years.
  • HTT · Huntingtin
    The primary disease gene. CAG expansion produces toxic mutant huntingtin protein.
